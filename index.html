The Science Behind Bromelain

Bromelain inhibits prostaglandin synthesis, reducing inflammation and pain, while stimulating plasmin to improve blood flow. Its efficacy in muscle relaxation makes it a compelling alternative for menstrual relief.

  • Natural anti‑inflammatory action
  • Muscle‑relaxing properties
  • No gastric side‑effects

Frequently Asked Questions

Is bromelain safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. Our pilot studies showed no irritation in 96% of participants; however, discontinue use if redness persists.

How long does relief last?

Most users report noticeable comfort within 15 minutes and lasting relief for 6–8 hours.

Is the patch compostable?

Absolutely. All materials—the hydrogel, backing film, and liner—break down in industrial compost within 90 days.
